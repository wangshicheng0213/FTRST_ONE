Introduction

Developmental Delay (DD) refers to the phenomenon of slowing down or abnormal sequence in the process of growth and development. Genetic studies, especially the recent genome-wide association study (GWAS), have found a number of DD related mutations (loci). However, how to effectively extract meaningful biological information from the genetic discovery of DD remains a major challenge. There is an urgent need to integrate multi-layer data from different sources, such as genetic discovery from GWAS, the relationship between disease and gene, research network for human genes, protein protein interaction (PPI), co expression, expression of quantitative trait loci (eQTL) and the performance of each gene at different stages, so as to provide comprehensive information for the transformation of genetic discovery into DD molecular diagnosis and mechanism research Combined resources. For this reason, we have developed dddb database, which is the comprehensive resource of DD research.
